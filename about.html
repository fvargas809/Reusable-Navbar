Reusable Apple-Style Nav Bar Template

This template showcases a clean, frosted-glass navigation experience inspired by Apple’s minimalist design. It’s built with Tailwind CSS utilities for fast customization and reuse across projects.

Reusable Structure

Drop the nav into any page and keep consistent spacing, typography, and effects.

Mega Menu

A full-width dropdown for structured navigation and clear category grouping.

Focus Effects

Content blurs and dims on hover, bringing attention to the menu.

Template Guidance

  • Replace the logo text with your brand name or SVG.
  • Update menu labels, descriptions, and links for your site.
  • Adjust opacity, blur, and spacing with Tailwind classes.
  • Extend sections below with your own content blocks.
